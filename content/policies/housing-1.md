heading: Housing Justice, for Renters and Owners
---
Detroiters pay the highest property tax rates in the state, which hit seniors and working families the hardest whether they own or rent. Unfair assessment and foreclosure practices have bankrupted Detroit's neighborhoods and sold the land to the highest bidder, all for tax bills as low as $80. Bryant will fight to repair the harm of unfair assessments, protect the rights of longtime residents, and create affordable pathways to renting and owning homes for new residents who want to live in a diverse community with justice for all and without gentrification.

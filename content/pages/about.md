    eyebrow: Passionate about Education
    body: >-
      Bryant is a limited license social worker and urban planner who is
      passionate about solving problems by addressing their root causes. A
      public school graduate and the son of two social workers, Bryant studied
      international relations at Michigan State University before teaching
      English for three years in Nablus, Palestine. Observing principled
      resistance to apartheid and working alongside local and international
      educators cemented Bryant's optimism that local communities with fewer
      resources can utilize a strengths-based approach to resist oppression and
      recognize human dignity.


      After 2020, Bryant returned to the United States to study Urban Planning and Social Work at the University of Michigan, where he participated in the Global Activities Scholars Program and the Dow Distinguished Award. He has taught English across the Middle East and North Africa, supported United Nations youth development work in Azerbaijan, worked on Palestinian-led environmental justice projects in Jordan and Israel, and helped facilitate environmental and zoning approvals for mutual aid housing projects in Brazil.
  - type: prose

      campaigned for progressive candidates in local elections, and served as a
      delegate at the 2026 Michigan Democratic Party Convention.
  - type: prose
    eyebrow: Career in Housing Solutions

      solutions-focused approach to representing the East Side, Midtown, and
      Downtown in the Michigan House of Representatives.
